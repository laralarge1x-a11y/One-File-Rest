### ANIMATIONS — APPLY EVERYWHERE

Use framer-motion for all of these:

1. **Page transitions** — every route change has fade + slide, wrapped in AnimatePresence in App.tsx
2. **List stagger** — when a list of items loads, each item animates in 0.08s after the previous
3. **Number count-up** — stat numbers animate from 0 to their value over 1 second when they enter the viewport
4. **Skeleton loading** — every data-fetching section shows animated skeleton screens (CSS shimmer animation) while loading, never a blank white flash
5. **Button feedback** — all buttons scale to 0.97 on click (active state)
6. **Card hover** — all cards lift 2px and shadow deepens on hover, smooth 200ms transition
7. **Form field focus** — border color transitions to accent, label floats up, subtle glow appears
8. **Success states** — checkmark SVG draws itself using stroke-dashoffset animation
9. **Error shake** — when form validation fails, input shakes horizontally (CSS keyframe)
10. **Modal/sheet open** — backdrop fades in, modal slides up or scales in
